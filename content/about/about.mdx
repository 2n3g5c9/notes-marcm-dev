## What is this place?

A public brain dump on some ideas I came across. It complements my private notes, hence deliberately
lacks context in favour of conciseness.

It also differs from a blog with no logical ordering, and I reserve the right to change and rearrange content anytime.

You shouldn't expect frequent updates as I'm fairly busy with other projects.

## How was it built?

Written from scratch in [React](https://reactjs.org/) and [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/) with
[Gatsby](https://www.gatsbyjs.com/) and [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/). No tracker is currently enabled on
the website.

You can find my [default starter on GitHub](https://github.com/2n3g5c9/gatsby-starter-typescript-tailwindcss), and learn
more about the context and process by reading the [note about how it was built](/notes/building-a-static-website-with-gatsby-and-typescript).
